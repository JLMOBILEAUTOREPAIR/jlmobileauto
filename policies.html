Service Partnership

JL MOBILE AUTO REPAIR & PERFORMANCE — COMMITMENT TO EXCELLENCE

Welcome to JL Mobile Auto. I bring dealer-level expertise directly to your location. To ensure the highest quality of work and clear communication, I operate under a set of professional guidelines that ensure you receive honest, efficient, and reliable service every time I arrive.

01 — Scheduling & Appointments

To reserve your priority time slot and manage travel logistics, a $50 booking deposit is required.

  • Value Applied: This deposit is not an extra fee; it is applied directly toward your final invoice upon completion.
  • Flexibility: If you need to reschedule, please notify me at least 24 hours in advance so we can keep your deposit active for a future date.

02 — Diagnostics & Professional Fees

My diagnostic fee ensures you receive a thorough, professional assessment of your vehicle’s health.

  • Transparent Billing: Travel and diagnostics are specialized services. These fees are maintained separately from repair costs to ensure you always see a clear, honest breakdown of the work performed.
  • Service Radius: Base fees cover my standard service area. Extended travel for out-of-area requests will always be disclosed and agreed upon before booking.

03 — Parts Quality & Sourcing

To guarantee the performance of your vehicle, I prefer to source high-quality components through my trusted commercial network.

  • My Guarantee: Parts I supply are backed by a commercial warranty, and I provide a comprehensive labor guarantee on that installation.
  • Customer-Supplied Parts: I am happy to install parts you provide, though I cannot offer a warranty on labor or product longevity for items I did not source. In these cases, a modest labor adjustment is applied to offset technical overhead.

04 — Vehicle Assessment

If your vehicle presents different symptoms upon arrival than originally described, we will pause to re-evaluate. My goal is to fix the root cause correctly, so we will pivot to a diagnostic flow to ensure you aren't paying for repairs that don't solve the problem.
