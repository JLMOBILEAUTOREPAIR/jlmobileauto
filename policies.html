JL MOBILE AUTO REPAIR & PERFORMANCE — BUSINESS POLICIES & TERMS OF SERVICE

Welcome to JL MOBILE AUTO REPAIR & PERFORMANCE. Because I bring a fully equipped mobile shop directly to your driveway or workplace, my operations differ slightly from a traditional brick-and-mortar garage. Please review my core policies regarding bookings, pricing, and warranties below.

01 — Booking Deposits & Cancellation Policy

To protect my schedule and cover immediate fuel and travel expenses, I require a non-refundable booking deposit to secure your mobile appointment slot.

  • The Deposit: A flat $50 deposit (or an amount equal to my base travel fee) is required via my secure payment link to lock in your time slot.
  • Applying the Deposit: This is not an extra fee. This deposit goes directly toward your final invoice upon completion of my service.
  • Cancellations & Rescheduling: If you need to reschedule, you must notify me at least 24 hours in advance to roll your deposit over to a new date. Cancellations or rescheduling requests made with less than 24 hours' notice, or "no-shows" where the vehicle or owner is unavailable upon arrival, will result in the forfeiture of the deposit.

02 — Service Call & Diagnostic Fee Policy

My mobile service call and diagnostic fee is a flat, non-refundable charge that covers my travel time, fuel, vehicle wear, and the first hour of professional pinpoint testing/troubleshooting.

  • No "Waived" Fees: Because travel and professional diagnostic expertise are separate, distinct expenses, my diagnostic and service call fees do not roll into or get waived by the cost of the subsequent repair.
  • Extended Travel: My base fee covers jobs within my primary service radius. Jobs outside my standard boundary may incur a tiered extended travel fee, which will be disclosed and approved prior to booking.

03 — Parts Matrix & Pricing Policy

I source high-quality, reliable parts through premium commercial vendors and dealerships to ensure your vehicle performs at its best.

  • Parts Matrix: Parts prices are billed using a standard industry sliding-scale matrix.
  • Price Ceilings: I benchmark my specialty and high-end components against local dealership retail counter pricing to ensure you are always charged a fair, competitive retail rate.

04 — Customer-Supplied Parts Policy

While I highly recommend letting me source your parts to ensure correct fitment and full warranty protection, I understand clients occasionally wish to supply their own.

  • Labor Surcharge: If you choose to supply your own parts, a premium surcharge of $15 – $25 per hour will be added to my standard labor rate to offset lost parts overhead.
  • NO PARTS OR LABOR WARRANTY: JL MOBILE AUTO REPAIR & PERFORMANCE provides absolutely zero warranty on labor, performance, or part longevity for customer-supplied parts.
  • RE-BILLABLE LABOR CHARGES: If a customer-supplied part fails, degrades over time, is found incorrect for the vehicle layout, or breaks during installation due to inherent factory defects, the customer is fully responsible for paying my regular standard labor rates again to perform the removal, swap, and re-installation processes.

05 — Warranty & Sourced Parts Protection

When I source your parts directly from my partner networks, I fully back the integrity of the execution.

  • My Guarantee: Parts sourced directly by me carry a standard commercial replacement warranty.
  • The Labor Guarantee: If a part I supplied fails within its warranty window, I absorb the operational labor cost to dispatch my mobile unit back out to your location and replace it. This complete peace of mind is structurally integrated right into my standard parts pricing.

06 — "No-Start" & Condition Modifications

If a vehicle is booked for a specific component replacement (e.g., brake pads) but upon arrival the vehicle cannot be safely operated, cannot crank, or has completely different underlying issues, the job will immediately pause and convert to a standard diagnostic fee structure until the true issue is identified.

By booking an appointment with JL MOBILE AUTO REPAIR & PERFORMANCE, you acknowledge and agree to the terms and business policies outlined above.
